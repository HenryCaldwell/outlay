What is Outlay?

Outlay is an innovative lifeguard management program designed to streamline the complex tasks involved in managing lifeguard teams. With Outlay, supervisors can effortlessly track lifeguard rotation changes, ensuring that times up and down from stands are properly optimized. The program facilitates optimal rotation orders and simplifies the assignment of rotations, making it easier to maintain a well-organized and efficient schedule. Additionally, Outlay provides easy access to and mutability of the rotation sheet, allowing for quick adjustments as needed, as well as built-in error prevention mechanisms to help avoid common scheduling mistakes and ensure accurate data managementThis ensures that lifeguard operations run smoothly, enhancing safety and efficiency at any aquatic facility.
